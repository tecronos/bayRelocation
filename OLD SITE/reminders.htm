Check your homeowner’s or renter’s insurance to see if your belongings are covered during the move.

To ensure service during transition, have PG&E and phone service turned on in your new place the day before you move in, and turned off at your old place the day after you move out.

It's very important to empty out all possible frozen liquids and ice cubes from your freezer before you vacate your home. If this isn't done, once PG&E shuts off your electricity the frozen liquids and ice cubes melt and leak onto your floors!

Refill important prescriptions to sustain you through the transition.

Order bank checks with your new address.

Have mail forwarded to new address. Obtain Change of Address Form at the Post Office, or update new address online at www.usps.gov.
