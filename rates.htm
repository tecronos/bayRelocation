Note: We have a 3 hour minimum for all moves.
1) Our Rates & Charges:
● Monday - Saturday: $120/h for 2 Men ; $180/h for 3 Men ; 240/hr for 4 men
● Billing time starts when our truck arrives at the Origin address, and ends once we are done moving you in at Destination.
● For moves within SF, 30 minutes is added for Return Drive to terminal. Additional driving charges may apply for moves outside of San Francisco.
● We have a 3 hour minimum charge. Quarter hour increments after first 3 hours.
● $15 fuel fee for moves within San Francisco. Add'l fuel fees will apply for moves outside of San Francisco.
● Plus wrapping materials used: tape, shrink, mattress covers, etc.
 
2) Notes:
● Wardrobes and Plastic Bins (10 of each), are provided at no charge and are available on moving day only.
● All trucks come with: "Floor Runners" + "blankets" + "dollies", in addition to custom made "Sofa Cover + Arm Chair Cover + TV Cover and Door Jam Pads".
● No hidden charges. We do not charge add'l for stairs, long carries, hoist procedures, bridge tolls, mileage, add'l pick-ups or drop-offs, assembly of furniture, etc.
● No additional charge for Pianos. We do not do single piano moves, rather we move pianos in conjunction to moving the entire household furniture.
 
3) Additional Info:
● Basic insurance is included. Additional Insurance is available upon customer request: About Insurance
● Parking in San Francisco: You are responsible for providing adequate parking. As a courtesy, if we are issued with a parking ticket, our company will absorb the first $50 per move. The remaining balance will be your responsibility.
● Certificate of Insurance is available for $20.
● Form of Payment: Cash, Check and Credit Card (5% convenience charge for cc payment) - No Billing! Also, CASH or CREDIT CARD ONLY may be required if your move is into storage; a last minute booking; moving away from the Bay Area.
● Our Cancellation Policy: We have a 5 day in advance written notice cancellation requirement. Please inquire for more information.
